Arjun was a curious boy who lived in a quiet village surrounded by forests and rivers. He loved exploring nature and asking questions about everything he saw.

Arjun had two brothers, Rohan and Kiran. Rohan was older and very responsible, always helping their parents with work. Kiran was younger and playful, often following Arjun around on his adventures.

Arjun’s best friend was Vikram. They had been friends since childhood and spent most of their time together. Vikram was brave and often encouraged Arjun to explore new places.

One day, while wandering near the forest, Arjun and Vikram discovered a hidden path that led to an ancient cave. The cave walls were covered with strange symbols and drawings that told stories of the past.

As they explored deeper, they found a glowing stone placed on a pedestal. When Arjun touched the stone, he suddenly began to see visions of the future — storms, floods, and dangers approaching the village.

At first, Arjun was scared, but with Vikram’s support, he decided to use this ability to help others. He warned the villagers about upcoming dangers, and many lives were saved because of him.

Meanwhile, his brothers also played important roles. Rohan helped organize the villagers and ensured everyone stayed safe. Kiran, although young, spread messages quickly across the village and helped gather supplies.

Over time, Arjun realized that each vision made him weaker. The more he used his power, the more exhausted he became. Vikram noticed this and grew worried.

One evening, Vikram told Arjun that he didn’t have to carry this burden alone. He reminded Arjun that true strength comes from working together.

In the final moment, Arjun had to make a difficult choice: continue using the stone and risk his life, or give up the power and live normally.

With the support of Vikram and his brothers, Arjun chose to destroy the stone. The visions stopped, but he felt free again.

From that day on, Arjun, Vikram, Rohan, and Kiran worked together to protect and improve their village, relying not on magic, but on teamwork and courage.
