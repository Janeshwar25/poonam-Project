Machine Learning is a field of artificial intelligence that focuses on enabling systems to learn from data. It is widely used in recommendation systems, fraud detection, and self-driving cars.

Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers. It is particularly powerful for image recognition and natural language processing.
